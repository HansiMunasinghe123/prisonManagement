Department of Prison

Department of Prison plays a huge role the in the law and reformation sector in Sri Lanka under Ministry of Justice and Law Reforms. Under the motto of Custody, Care and Correction, Correction plays a major role in this institute. Correction is the finest procedure to mitigate crimes in a country. Therefore, officers related to correction or rehabilitation carries one of the massive responsibilities in the law and reformation sector. The officers working in this section are also well trained, educated officers. But when it comes to the administration of these officers the commissioner of rehabilitation and admin officers had lots of issues.
